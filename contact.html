FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out to our team.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

Our solutions team reviews every inquiry within 24 business hours. For urgent projects, feel free to call us directly — we pick up.

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes, completely. The 30-minute discovery call has zero cost and zero commitment. It's our chance to understand your vision and share how we can help.

What happens after I submit the inquiry?

One of our scope engineers will review your submission, prepare a tailored response with estimated timelines and budgets, and schedule a kickoff call.

Do you sign NDAs before discussions?

Absolutely. We treat every project idea as confidential. We're happy to sign an NDA before any detailed technical discussions if you prefer.

Can you work with an existing codebase?

Yes. We offer code audits, refactoring, and incremental development on existing apps. Just describe your current stack in the inquiry form.

What's the minimum project budget?

Our engagements typically start at $15,000 for a focused MVP. We're happy to discuss phased approaches for startups with tighter initial budgets.
