Our Story

From a 3-Person Startup to a 50+ Team Agency

AppForge was founded in 2013 by three software engineers who were frustrated by the gap between what mobile apps could be and what most development shops were actually building. We started with a simple belief: great mobile experiences are built by teams that truly care about the end user.

Today, we've delivered 200+ apps across 15+ industries, helping startups raise millions and enterprises serve billions of users. Our proprietary client portal — born from our own frustrations with project opacity — is now the cornerstone of every engagement.

  • Real-time project dashboards for every client
  • 2-week agile sprints with weekly client reviews
  • Full-stack teams — design, dev, QA, and DevOps
  • End-to-end App Store submission and post-launch SLA
